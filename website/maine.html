Paw Facts

The Maine Coon is a large cat that matches their amazing size with a strong, athletic physique. They have big, pointed ears that are held high and wide, along with a perceptive expression. The coat is thick and long, with eye-catching tufts on the paws and ears and an undercoat protected by a thick, glossy, waterproof top coat. Stunning, their tails ought to be at least as long as their bodies so they can use them to wrap their bodies in extra insulation. Maine Coon cats are affectionate, lively, and sociable. They adore human interaction, which makes them ideal companions for anyone who enjoys sharing their home with such a large cat. Maine Coon cats are known for their lovely quiet chirping sound.
