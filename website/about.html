What is this website about?

This website was made with the intention of being an informative and fun experience for the viewers. It provides random facts about cats to pique the interest of the viewers that they may or may not have already known. If they are interested and want to learn more about a cat, they can within this website. They can also hear how they sound like and see more pictures of them.
